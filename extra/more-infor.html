    Your Google Cloud Service Account does not have the correct permissions to enable Precog to write data.

    Go to https://cloud.google.com then make sure you are in the desired Google Cloud project. Click the menu at the top left, then click IAM & Admin, then click Service Accounts.

    Click Create Service Account, name the account Precog then click Create And Continue. Next click Select a role and type "BigQuery User" then click BigQuery User (not BigQuery Job User or BigQuery Read Session User). Next click the white "Continue" button then click "Done".

    Click the service account email e.g. precog@example.iam.gserviceaccount.com then click Keys, click "Add key", click "Create new key" then click "Create".

    Note that billing needs to be enabled in your Google Cloud project.

    In your Precog pipeline click "Edit destination" then upload the file which was downloaded in the previous step, then try again.
